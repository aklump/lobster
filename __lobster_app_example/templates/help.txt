usage: __lobster_app_example <command> [<args>]

The most commonly used __lobster_app_example commands are:
   php        Example of a php handler located at routes/php.php
   bash       Example of a bash handler located at routes/bash.sh
   help       Example of a text handler, a.k.a., theme located at templates.help.txt

Calling '__lobster_app_example' without argument is equivalent to '__lobster_app_example help '
